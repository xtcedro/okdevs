Our Mission

At OKDEVS, we aim to create a thriving community of developers, technologists, and innovators in Oklahoma. Our mission is to empower individuals through open-source technologies, collaborative projects, and educational resources. By bridging gaps in accessibility and fostering collaboration, we strive to bring meaningful change to both technology and our community.

Whether you're an experienced developer, a tech enthusiast, or just beginning your coding journey, OKDEVS is here to provide opportunities for growth, innovation, and connection. Together, we can build a stronger, more inclusive tech future.
